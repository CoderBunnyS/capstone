Catherine Smith

Director of ABA operations

Catherine is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) and has accumulated more than 10 years of experience in the field of ABA working with children. While most of her experience has been in the area of early intervention using the verbal behavior approach, she also has extensive experience teaching daily living and independent skills, social skills, functional communication, and crisis intervention across multiple settings. She has held multiple management and operations positions within an organization while continuing to provide direct care to the clients she supervised. In addition to her passion for working with children who have a diagnosis of Autism Spectrum Disorder, Catherine also has strong interests in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy and applying the ABA principles to health, sports, and fitness goals. Outside of work, Catherine enjoys being active outdoors, traveling, and spending time with her dogs and cats.
